Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Orb One™?

Orb One™ is a new type of personal device designed to help you focus, connect, and manage your digital life without compromising your privacy. Think of it as a mindful companion that puts your well-being first, not a device that demands your constant attention.

2. How is it different from a smartphone or smart speaker?

The key difference is our philosophy. Unlike other devices that are built to collect your data and keep you scrolling, Orb One™ is built for "Radical Trust." It operates with privacy-first principles, gives you complete control over your data, and is designed to minimize distractions, not create them.

3. How do you protect my privacy?

Privacy isn't an afterthought for us; it's our foundation. All data is processed on the device whenever possible, and any data that must be stored is encrypted and under your exclusive control. We are committed to transparency and will provide clear, simple documentation on how every piece of data is handled.

4. When will Orb One™ be available?

We are in the pre-launch phase and are currently building our waitlist for early adopters. We are targeting our first pilot programs for late 2026. By joining the waitlist, you'll be the first to know about our progress and the first to have an opportunity to pre-order.

5. How can I support the project?

Joining the waitlist is the best way to show your support! It helps us demonstrate demand to early partners and investors. We also encourage you to share our mission with friends and family who believe in a healthier future for technology.
